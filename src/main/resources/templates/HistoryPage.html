Our History

SKG was founded in 2021 by JASKARAN SINGH, ANURAG KUMAR and ARIJIT GUHA in Jamshedpur, a small town in Jharkhand with a single truck and a vision that was way ahead of its time. SKG gradually expanded its services to Jamshedpur, Ranchi and Dhanbad. From this humble beginning SKG has today grown into a nationally renowned logistics and transport company which is also currently the largest fleet owner of commercial vehicles in India with a fleet of 121 Vehicles.

Over the years, SKG has pioneered in providing a safe and reliable delivery network in the field of parcel service. It has spread its operations to Courier Service, Priority Cargo and Transport of Passengers by Air to meet the growing demands of its burgeoning customer base.

3PL and Warehousing solutions offered by SKG are tailor-made and cater to unique needs of its diverse customer base. With the largest goods transportation network in India, SKG parcel service is indispensable for a large number of Corporate Houses. This network spans the length and breadth of the country and is supported by strategically located transshipment hubs. We operate through a network of 52 Branch and franchisees to cater our valuable customers and we are now in the process of expanding our services to reach even the remotest locations of the Country.
